Inuit

Clothing

The Inuit's wore Caribou and sealskin clothing. These easily avalible materials have helped the Inuit's to survive through the climates! Caribou skin are used for summer tents and a cusion for the sled(Qamutik). There are other things that the Inuit's made using Caribou skin.

What They Ate

Some main food sources that the Inuit's ate, were Artic Char, seal, Polar Bear, and Caribou. The foods provided lots of vitamens and nutrietions, to stay warm and healthy.
